Why Snake Plants Are So Popular

Snake plants are loved because they:

  • tolerate low light
  • survive missed waterings
  • grow slowly
  • improve indoor air quality
  • look great in modern spaces

But "hardy" doesn't mean invincible.

Light Requirements

Snake plants prefer bright, indirect light, but adapt to lower light.

Avoid:

  • harsh midday sun
  • dark corners long-term

Signs of light trouble:

  • pale leaves = too dark
  • scorched patches = too sunny
