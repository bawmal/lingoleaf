This Privacy Policy describes how LingoLeaf ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of the LingoLeaf smart plant care product, mobile application, and website (collectively, the "Service").

We are committed to handling your personal data in an open and transparent manner and strictly adhering to regulations regarding the use of your mobile information.

IMPORTANT NOTICE ON ACCESSIBILITY

A direct, static link to this policy is available for end-users and is included within all mobile messaging opt-in flows (e.g., in the consent message or sign-up form disclosure).

1. Information We Collect

To provide our personalized plant care advice and service communications, we collect several types of information:

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Data: Personal identifiers such as your name, email address, and a hashed password.
  • Plant Profile Data: Information you enter about your plants, including plant type (e.g., succulent), pot size, soil type, and observed health status.
  • Mobile Messaging Data (Mandatory): Your mobile phone number is collected if you opt-in to receive mobile text messages from us.

B. Data Collected by the LingoLeaf Device/App

  • Sensor Data: Real-time and historical measurements from the LingoLeaf device, including soil moisture levels (e.g., to determine "bone dry" state), temperature, and humidity.
  • Light Tracking Data: Data related to the plant's exposure, including directional light intensity and duration (e.g., west-facing window).
  • Location Data: We collect general location data (city and state, e.g., Phoenix, AZ) to factor in regional climate, sun intensity, and evaporative conditions for accurate watering recommendations. We do not collect precise GPS location without your explicit permission.
  • Usage/Algorithm Data: Interactions with the app, device ID, watering frequency, and the output of the care algorithm.

2. Use of Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following primary purposes:

  • Service Provision & Personalization: To run our proprietary care algorithm and generate tailored watering and light recommendations based on your sensor readings and climate data.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage and improve the accuracy and efficacy of our care algorithms and platform performance.
  • Communication: To send you service-related notifications and updates.
